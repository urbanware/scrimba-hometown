Visit Toronto

The world lives here

Largest city in Canada with the diversity to match. While an English speaking city, we also have 150 languages and cultures represented. This makes Toronto one of the most multicultural cities in the world. Visit the Toronto islands, parks, restaurants, cinema, and more. Summers at the beach or Winters in the movie theatres, doesn't matter the season! You are welcome here. Cheers!

Top three activities to do in Toronto

Toronto Islands

Take the ferry and explore the islands on foot or by bike. Bike ferry is called Hanlan's Point.

Movies and Restaurants

Major studio releases, international film festivals, we love subtitles! From basic hotdog stands to the cuisines of the world. We got you covered.

Blue Jays

Cheer on our local team. Are we on a winning or losing streak? Are we not entertained? You might see the world's first fully retractable roof in motion!
